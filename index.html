One day, I have been on the holidays at ≈Åeba. I was walking on the street which is next to the beach, when I saw many go-karts. You can rent them if you have eighteen years old or someone adult is with you. I remember that I went fast to my parents and told them about it. Then I came back with one of them and bought one hour to drive this amazing ,,car". It was great! I felt like I really drove a car, because in fact, I didnt know how real cars work.
